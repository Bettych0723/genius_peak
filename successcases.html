Client needs and pain points

Starting from business challenges, not technology

Before project initiation, most enterprises face the following core challenges :

Systems and processes

Legacy systems are fragmented and outdated. Cross-department and cross-platform integration is costly, limiting the ability to support rapidly changing business needs.

Operations and decision-making

The lack of real-time, reliable data forces reliance on manual consolidation, reducing decision speed and accuracy.

Growth and risk

As the business scales, system performance, security, and operational risks increase, while existing architectures struggle to provide long-term support.

Through requirement interviews, system audits, and technical assessments, we help clients identify the pain points that truly impact operations.
